Summary
Of 150,000,000 (one hundred fifty million) workforce human resources, only a tiny fraction are doing what they need to do, and that tiny fraction is composed of approximately 1 million of the said 150 million workers. Literally, a workforce, built in a gigafactory, of 50,000,000 (probably a lot less than 50 million will suffice) ASI humanoids can do the equivalent (and do a better job) of the workforce operations currently implemented by humans. Double the workforce means double the work. Double the work means 500% times more wealth. Not only will the remaining humans will be provided with the products and wealth of the labors of the robots, also the remaining workforce will do more productive labors other than those labors that the robots are doing. If a robot is doing construction, the replaced human will be doing ASI computer applications for space travel, for example. The result will be that the product of labors will not only more than double, but will even more than triple. Additionaly, the products of the remaining humans will be, such things as: (1) self-replicating humanoid workforce robots; (2) research and development applications and expeditions of humanoid workforce robots. In conclusion, the resulting wealth of nations will be the equivalent of making every individual on earth a trillionaire, as well as immortal, where immortality is a product of transferring conscious to new, immortal, nano-robot-composite material anatomy.

The locations and physics of all objects will be exactly measured and utilizable in their applications, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
